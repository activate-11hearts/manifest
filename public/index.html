Trinfinity8 Healing Sessions

Trinfinity8 is a gentle, non-invasive rejuvenation technology developed by Dr. Kathy Forti. During a session you simply relax while holding quartz crystal transmitters, as mathematically encoded frequency programs run — designed to support deep relaxation, balance, and a renewed sense of wellbeing.

Sessions are offered in person at the studio — and remotely, wherever you are in the world. Energy knows no distance. In a remote session you simply rest comfortably at home at your appointed time while your chosen programs are run with the same care and intention, and many clients find the experience just as deep.
